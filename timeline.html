Presenting Nadia Comaneci and her Incredible Journey!

  • November 12, 1961

    Nadia Comaneci is born.

  • 1967

    She starts gymnastics.

  • 1976

    Montreal Olympics: She scores the first perfect ten.

  • 1980

    Participates in Moscow Olympics.

  • 1984

    She retires from competition.

  • 1989

    She moves to the Unites States.

  • 1993

    Nadia Comaneci is inducted to the International Gymnastics Hall of Fame.

  • 1996

    She married Bart Conner.

  • 1999

    She is honored by ABC News and Ladies' Home Journal as one of the 100 Most Important Women of the 20th Century.

  • 2001

    She becomes an American citizen.
